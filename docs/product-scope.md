# Product Scope

## Product Name

Working name: **MidwayOS**

MidwayOS is a lightweight operating system for Midway: public website, RV bookings, Square payments, inventory visibility, accounting offload, staff tools, and AI-assisted operations.

The product should eventually be reusable for similar local stores, RV parks, camp stores, small markets, and gas/convenience businesses.

## Primary Users

### Customers

Customers want to:

- Know if Midway is open.
- Find directions quickly.
- Call or text.
- See what Midway carries.
- Book an RV site without friction.
- Understand site amenities before paying.
- Pay securely.
- Receive a clear confirmation.

### Owner

The owner wants to:

- See today's business at a glance.
- Manage bookings from a phone.
- Know which RV sites are available, booked, blocked, arriving, or departing.
- See sales, low stock, and urgent alerts.
- Update website content without a developer.
- Use Square as much as possible instead of duplicating work.
- Push accounting/admin work out of the owner's day.
- See only exceptions instead of reconciling every sale, payout, tax, fee, and refund.
- Ask AI questions about the business.
- Approve AI actions safely.

### Employees

Employees want to:

- Check in guests.
- Answer availability questions.
- Update inventory counts.
- See simple instructions.
- Avoid complex tools.
- Avoid seeing owner-only financial or configuration data unless permitted.

### Future Operators

If MidwayOS becomes reusable, another store owner would want:

- The same admin system with different branding.
- Different site sections enabled/disabled.
- Their own Square location.
- Their own inventory categories.
- Their own map, rooms, sites, slips, rentals, or assets.

## Important Convenience Store Owner Needs

Beyond the website and RV booking, a convenience store owner often needs:

- Fuel price updates.
- Fuel tank level visibility and reorder reminders.
- Low-stock alerts for fast-moving products.
- Vendor reorder drafts.
- Shift notes and employee handoff notes.
- Incident log for maintenance, customer issues, refunds, or safety concerns.
- Expiring item reminders.
- Ice, firewood, propane, bait, and seasonal inventory visibility.
- Daily close checklist.
- Cash drawer or POS close summary references.
- Weather-aware alerts for snow, road conditions, camping weekends, holidays, and pass traffic.
- Local event awareness for staffing and inventory planning.
- Seasonal demand forecasting.
- Accounting-ready daily summaries.
- Square payout reconciliation.
- QuickBooks/accountant packet generation.
- Exception-only bookkeeping review.
- Simple task lists for employees.
- Customer FAQ answers.
- Reputation management links for Google reviews.
- Google Business Profile consistency.
- Photo/news update flow for Instagram and website announcements.
- Maintenance calendar for pumps, freezers, espresso machine, ice machine, RV hookups, septic, water, and signage.

## MVP Scope

MVP should include:

- Public single-page Midway site.
- Local SEO foundation.
- Instagram embed.
- Call, text, directions.
- RV property map.
- Site-specific amenities.
- Booking calendar.
- Full upfront Square payment.
- Booking confirmation.
- Owner/employee dashboard login.
- Booking management.
- Basic content management.
- Fuel price and hours management.

## Out of Scope for MVP

These are important but should come after the first release:

- SMS automation.
- Full inventory ordering workflow.
- Barcode scanning.
- AI placing vendor orders.
- Live QuickBooks sync.
- Advanced sales forecasting.
- Multi-tenant theming for other businesses.
- Customer support chatbot.
- Loyalty/rewards.
- Full ecommerce store.
