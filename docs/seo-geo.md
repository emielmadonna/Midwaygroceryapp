Midway should be easy to find for people in and around Plain, Washington, especially visitors traveling through Leavenworth, Lake Wenatchee, and Stevens Pass.

## On-Site SEO

MVP:

- Strong title and meta description.
- LocalBusiness schema.
- GasStation schema if accurate.
- Campground/RV schema where appropriate.
- Address, phone, hours.
- Google Maps link.
- Image alt text.
- Fast-loading pages.
- Open Graph metadata.
- Sitemap.
- Robots file.

Later:

- FAQ schema.
- Seasonal pages.
- Service pages for RV sites, bait/tackle, ice, fuel, Lake Wenatchee supplies.

## Local Business Listings

Owner should eventually confirm:

- Google Business Profile.
- Apple Business Connect.
- Bing Places.
- Yelp if relevant.
- Local tourism/directory listings.

NAP consistency:

- Name
- Address
- Phone

These must match everywhere.

## Content Notes

Plain Valley positioning:

- Plain is close to Leavenworth, Lake Wenatchee, and Stevens Pass.
- Visitors come for year-round recreation.
- Midway can serve camping, fishing, lake, ski, road-trip, and local convenience needs.

Do not overstuff keywords. Write useful local copy.
