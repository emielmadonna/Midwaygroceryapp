Your basecamp for the Chiwawa valley.

Midway isn't a parking lot off the interstate — it's a working gas and grocery store in the trees at Plain, Washington, with full-hookup RV sites out back. Roll in after a day on the trail or the river, top off the tank, grab groceries and a soft serve, and settle in by the fire. Everything you forgot to pack is already on the shelf.

Questions, answered.

What hookups are included at the RV sites?

Our RV sites are full-hookup, with power, water and sewer at the site. Call us with your rig length and we'll match you to a site that fits.

How close is it to Leavenworth and Lake Wenatchee?

Midway sits on Chiwawa Loop Road in Plain, Washington — just minutes from downtown Leavenworth and right on the road toward Lake Wenatchee, making it a handy base camp for the whole Chiwawa River valley.

Are pets allowed?

Yes — well-behaved, leashed pets are welcome at Midway. Please clean up after them and keep them with you at your site.

Is there fuel and a store on site?

Yes. Midway is a working gas and grocery store with 24/7 fuel and diesel at the pump, espresso and soft serve, groceries, beer & wine, ice, firewood and propane — all steps from your site.

How do I book an RV site?

Check live availability and reserve online from our booking page, or call us at (509) 596-1076 and we'll get you set up.
