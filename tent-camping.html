Questions, answered.

Are campfires allowed, and is firewood available?

Tent sites have a fire pit, and we sell bundled firewood right in the store. Fires are subject to seasonal burn bans — when the county or forest issues fire restrictions, please follow them.

How close is it to Leavenworth and Lake Wenatchee?

Midway is on Chiwawa Loop Road in Plain, Washington — just minutes from Leavenworth and right on the road toward Lake Wenatchee, so you're close to town but tucked into the trees.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes — leashed, well-behaved pets are welcome at the tent sites. Please keep them with you and clean up after them.

What's on site for campers?

A full gas and grocery store: 24/7 fuel, espresso and soft serve, groceries, beer & wine, ice, firewood, propane and bait & tackle — everything you forgot to pack is steps from your tent. There's also Wi-Fi and portable restrooms on site.

How do I book a tent site?

Check live availability and reserve online from our booking page, or call us at (509) 596-1076.
